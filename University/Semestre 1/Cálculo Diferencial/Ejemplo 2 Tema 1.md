Evalúa:
$$ 
\lim_{x \to 2} { x^2 + 3x - 10 \over x^2 + x - 6}
$$
$$
= {2^2 + 3(2) - 10 \over 2^2 + 2 - 6}
$$
$$
= {4+6-10 \over 2^2 + 2 - 6} = {0 \over 0}
$$
Alternativa con factorización:
$$
= \lim_{x \to 2} {(x+5)(x-2) \over (x+3)(x-2)} = \lim_{x \to 2} {(x+5) \over (x+3)}
$$
$$
= \lim_{x\to2} {x+5\over x+3} = {2+5\over x+3} = {7\over5} = 1.4
$$
